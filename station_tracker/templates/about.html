About Us

Welcome to FUEL BUDDY! YOUR ULTIMATE GAS PRICE TRACKER app which is an online platform designed to assist customers/consumers in monitoring and comparing the fuel prices at various gas stations in their local area which is a reliable way to stay updated about the fluctuations in fuel prices. The app allows them to make choices that help save money on fuel and save time also with location-based search users can quickly find nearby gas stations with real-time price updates. The goal is to provide consumers with convenience while also encouraging price transparency and competition among gas stations. This application meets the demands of both consumers and fuel companies. It enables users to make more cost-effective decisions, save money and time. This application can help gas station owners by monitoring their competitors pricing strategies in real-time, ensuring they have an adequate supply of fuel on hand, responding to market changes, and understanding consumer preferences from feedback.
